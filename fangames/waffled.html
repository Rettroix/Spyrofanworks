Waffled's 2D Spyro Project
By: Waffled
  Spyro fangame Created by Waffled This is currently the best 2D Spyro game that has amazing design and really feels spyro-ey despite being 2D. So far, there is 5 levels, consisting of one hub, three levels and a boss level. There are a total of 500 Gems to collect as well as 10 Orbs. At least 5 of these orbs are needed to access the boss fight via the golden whirlwind at then end of the hub world.

There is a demo on the website and development can be followed on youtube.
